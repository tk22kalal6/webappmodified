RG Kar Rape Appeal

January 2025

Sanjay Roy, convicted in the brutal August 9, 2024 rape-murder of a postgraduate trainee at Kolkata's RG Kar Medical College, has appealed his life sentence in the Calcutta High Court.

  • The CBI and state sought a death penalty
  • Roy's defense cited "reformative and rehabilitative" grounds
  • Hearing scheduled for July 16, 2025
Background: The 2024 incident triggered nationwide doctor strikes due to safety concerns. The former principal and a police officer were also later arrested for evidence tampering.

Udaipur Electrocution Tragedy

June 2025

On June 18, 2025, Dr Ravi Sharma, a senior resident at RNT Medical College Udaipur, died after electrocution from a faulty water cooler in hostel premises.

  • Resident doctors launched an indefinite strike demanding accountability and compensation
  • Strike resumed after 11 days following preliminary assurances
  • Safety concerns raised about hostel infrastructure
Impact: This incident highlighted the urgent need for better safety measures in medical college hostels across the country.

Jabalpur Ragging Suicide

June 2025

A first-year student at Netaji Subhash Chandra Medical College in Jabalpur died by suicide, allegedly due to ragging; jumped from the 4th floor.

  • Family alleged ragging via phone call
  • Police are investigating both ragging and possible personal-relationship issues
  • Anti-ragging committees under scrutiny
Action Taken: The college has suspended several students pending investigation and strengthened anti-ragging measures.

₹1,000 Cr Medical College Scam

Ongoing Investigation

CBI has exposed one of India's largest medical-college scams involving high-level officials and middlemen, allegedly receiving crores in bribes for illegal accreditations.

  • Estimated bribery amount: ₹1,000-1,300 Cr
  • Over 40 colleges across multiple states involved
  • National Medical Commission (NMC) has frozen all new medical-college approvals
  • Seat increases for 2025-26 pending investigation
Impact: This scandal has shaken the medical education sector and raised questions about the integrity of the accreditation process.

Kottayam Ragging Horror

February 2025

On February 11, 2025, five senior students at the Government College of Nursing, Kottayam, Kerala violently assaulted a junior student in a shocking ragging incident.

  • Seniors tied, slashed, and stabbed junior with a metal divider
  • Applied lotion to wounds to hide evidence
  • The assault went viral via video
  • FIR registered and five accused were arrested and suspended
Legal Action: All five seniors have been charged under relevant sections and face potential expulsion from the nursing program.
